# TELOS Example

This is a sample TELOS document to illustrate how to structure your own. Adapt and expand based on your actual situation.

## Problems

- I feel most people spend their entire careers executing other people's visions instead of creating their own
- Existing AI tools are too technical and exclude non-programmers from the AI revolution
- Good ideas die because people don't have systems to capture and develop them
- Knowledge is trapped in individual brains instead of being shared and built upon
- Most learning resources are too theoretical and don't connect to real problems

## Goals

- Help more people transition from passive consumers to active creators
- Make personal AI infrastructure accessible to non-technical users
- Create systems that help people capture, develop, and share their ideas
- Build a community of people learning and growing together
- Make knowledge sharing frictionless and rewarding

## Challenges

- **Technical**: Many target users don't know command-line interfaces or programming
- **Resource**: Limited time to build everything I want to build
- **Psychological**: Imposter syndrome - feeling like I'm not "expert enough" to share publicly
- **Systemic**: Existing tooling ecosystems don't support my vision
- **Attention**: Competing with infinite distractions for people's time and attention

## Strategies

- Start with open source projects to lower barriers to entry
- Use Markdown files instead of databases for portability and simplicity
- Solve my own problems first, then scale solutions to others
- Share everything I learn - "learn out loud" model
- Build tools that work for me first - if they help me, they'll help others
- Focus on enabling human creativity rather than replacing it
- Create content that compounds over time (evergreen knowledge)

## Workflows

### Daily Capture
- **Morning walk**: Use Limitless app to voice-record ideas and thoughts
- **After walk**: AI processes and organizes recordings into topics
- **Review**: Flag items that need development or sharing

### Content Creation
- **Ideation**: Use "parliamentary debate" AI personas to challenge ideas from multiple angles
- **Drafting**: AI creates initial draft based on my voice and style
- **Refinement**: My edits focus on clarity and personal touch
- **Publishing**: One-click posting to X and LinkedIn
- **Archive**: Content automatically tagged and stored for future reference

### Research & Learning
- **Discovery**: YouTube, books, papers on topics of interest
- **Processing**: AI summarizes and connects to existing knowledge base
- **Integration**: Identify how new knowledge changes my TELOS
- **System Update**: Modify projects and workflows based on insights

### Technical Work
- **Session setup**: Clear definition of specific goal
- **Documentation**: Real-time markdown notes of decisions and rationale
- **Testing**: Every change verified before moving on
- **Commit**: Atomic commits with clear messages
- **Review**: Weekly retrospective on what worked and didn't
